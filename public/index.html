How To Play Rhyme Rush

Come up with rhymes that match the target word to score points before the timer runs out!

  • Type one word at a time in the text box and hit enter to submit
  • Words that are phonetically closer are given extra bonus points

Rhyme Category Examples:

Perfect Rhymes exactly match the stressed vowel onward
(10 points + phonetic similarity bonus)

  • "Rhyme" - Time, Climb, Thyme, Prime
  • "Beginner" - Dinner, Inner, Winner

Off Rhymes share consonants but not vowels
(7 points + phonetic bonus)

  • "Rhyme" - Ram, Room, Rum, Dream, Frame
  • "Beginner" - Bargainer, Gainer, Goner

Slant Rhymes share vowels but not consonants
(5 points + phonetic bonus)

  • "Rhyme" - Rice, Buy, Fire, Night, Find
  • "Beginner" - Bewilder, Deliver, Elixir

Near Rhymes are other close matches
(1 to 3 points)

  • "Rhyme" - Realm, Ring, Reign, Limb, Tome, Writhe
  • "Beginner" - Begonia, Demeanor, Burglar
