How to Play Rhyme Rush

Objective: Match words that rhyme with the given target word to score points. You're racing against the clock, so be quick!

Basic Steps:

  1. Type & Submit: Enter one word at a time in the text box and hit enter to submit it.
  2. Scoring: Earn more points for closer rhymes. Bonus points are awarded based on phonetic similarity.

Rhyme Categories & Points:

  1. Perfect Rhymes (10 pts + bonus): Exactly matches from the stressed vowel sound onwards.
    Examples:
    • "Rhyme" → Time, Climb, Thyme
    • "Beginner" → Dinner, Inner, Winner
  2. Off Rhymes (7 pts + bonus): Shared consonants, different vowels.
    Examples:
    • "Rhyme" → Ram, Room, Dream
    • "Beginner" → Bargainer, Goner
  3. Slant Rhymes (5 pts + bonus): Shared vowels, different consonants.
    Examples:
    • "Rhyme" → Rice, Buy, Night
    • "Beginner" → Bewilder, Deliver
  4. Near Rhymes (1-3 pts): Other closely matching sounds but not exactly alike.
    Examples:
    • "Rhyme" → Realm, Ring
    • "Beginner" → Begonia, Demeanor
